Timeline of my Life

  • Early Life

    I was born on December 11, 2002, in Idaho Falls, Idaho. My father was a teacher and my mother stayed home. We moved from Idaho to Flint, Michigan, where we lived for 6 years.

  • Tumulutous Times

    During the 2009 recession, my dad lost his teaching job. We moved to Denver, Colorado, where my dad found new work. A year later we packed up and headed to Greeley so my dad could attend Colorado State University.

  • A New Start

    After graduating with a Doctorate in Choral Conducting, my dad found employment in Valley City, North Dakota. We moved there in the summer of 2012 and lived there for 10 years. During that time I attended Valley City High School and was accepted to Brigham Young University.

  • See You Later

    After graduating high school in 2021, I began attending BYU majoring in accounting. After completing my freshman year on the Marriott School's Dean's List, I took a sales job with Aptive Environmental in Reston, Virginia.

  • The Next Chapter

    After taking a semester off of school, I returned to BYU in the winter of 2023. During the semester I was called to be a missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints in Scottsdale Arizona. I will serve there until May 2025.
